Are you doing these at-home workouts to burn belly fat?

If burning belly fat and building six-pack abs were easy things to do, everyone would have done it. Ask someone who has worked restlessly in the gym to achieve a toned and healthy body, you will come to realize how difficult it is to get rid of those stubborn belly fat. This extra fat is not only difficult to lose but also poses a great risk for health. So, if you are among those who are wondering how to get rid of belly fat, you have come to the right place.

Our goal here is simple, to provide you with the best and practical ways to burn stomach fat without making things complicated for you.

At-home workouts to burn belly fat

Check out these simple workouts and see the results!

Running and walking

Yes, this might would easy but moving your legs is a proven and easy way to get rid of those love handles. There is no single way to target belly fat and the key here is to keep your body moving. When you walk or run, it helps in burning calories, and your body's fat percentage decreases. These simple exercises help in reducing belly fat as well as fat from the other areas of your body.

Burpee

When it comes to losing belly fat, it is important to work as many muscles as you can and burpee is an exercise that does exactly what is needed

Get ready for a head-to-toe workout. In a simple way, you need to go from a push-up position to a jump and then you need to get back to the push-up position.

This is a full-body calisthenics workout that also adds strength and endurance to your lower and upper body.

Medicine ball slam

Stand straight, keep your hands in front of you while holding the medicine ball, take the ball over your head until your hands get straight. Now, it is time to take out all your frustration by slamming the ball on the ground, squat down towards the ground by following the ball. From your core and shoulders to your lats and haps, the medicine ball slam is a full-body workout that also strengthens your cardiovascular system.

Dumbbell overhead lunge

During a lunge, add a dumbbell overhead, and when you shift load with every rep, the weight should be kept above you and all the muscles in your torso need to work together.

It is a stability exercise and this workout lengthens and contracts your core muscles. Your feet should be kept shoulder-width apart, your knees should be bent slightly. Do not forget to inhale when you raise the dumbbell above your head.

Exhale while moving into the deep lunge position. Switch sides and repeat, perform 10 reps with both legs.

Mountain climbers

This is a great powder move, and some people can find it challenging, especially in the beginning. This is a great workout that ramps up your heart rate, challenges and improves your balance and agility, a complete workout for your arms and core.

Being a calorie-burning workout that targets your core, this is a perfect workout to lose that stubborn belly fat while revealing your abs.

Get into a high plank position keeping your wrist directly under your shoulders. Draw your belly button in towards the spine and keep your core tight. Bring your right leg toward your chest and take it back in the plank position, repeat the same with your left leg
