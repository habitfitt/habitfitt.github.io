6 simple cardio exercises

If you are among those who think workout at home is difficult or doesn't give results, you need to re-think! There are various fitness enthusiasts who workout at home and still reap the desired results. In fact, if you do some exercises at home, there are some amazing additional benefits. Not only it will save your precious time but also allow you to choose your own schedule. Here, we bring you some of the most effective cardio exercises at home that can help you stay fit and healthy. 

Cardio exercises you can do at home

Check out these easy to perform exercises.

High knees 

One of the easiest cardio exercises that can be done in three easy steps, high knees is very effective and needs limited space.

  • Stand and keep your legs together, and keep your arms at your side. 
  • Lift your knee and bring it to your chest, repeat with both legs.
  • Pump your arms up and down and keep your legs going.

Butt kicks

Another simple exercise and simply the opposite of what you did in the previous exercise, instead of lifting your knees up high, you need to lift your heels up towards your butt.

  • Stand in the same position like high knees and keep your arms at your side 
  • Bring one heel towards your butt and bring it down, repeat with both legs.
  • Pump your arms and keep repeating with both legs.

Jumping jacks 

If you want to enjoy a full-body workout, then jumping jacks is the best choice to make.

  • Stand straight while keeping your legs together, your arms should be at your side.
  • Slightly bend your knees and jump and spread your legs (a little wider than your shoulders), your arms will be lifted overhead.
  • Jump back to center and repeat.

Lateral shuffles

In addition to improving your side-to-side coordination, lateral shuffles increase your heart rate, it's an amazing cardio workout.

  • Stand keeping your knees and hips bent, lean forward slightly, and brace your core. 
  • Push off your left foot and lift the right one, maintain your form, and move right.
  • Now, bring your feet closer together and shuffle to the right. 
  • Do the same process to the left side.

Crab walk

Crab walk is one of the easiest ways to keep your blood flowing and it adds strength to your core, arms, and legs.

  • You need to sit on the floor keeping your keens bent and feet flat. Your hands should be under your shoulder on the floor.
  • Evenly distribute your weight between arms and legs, lift your hips off the floor, and walk!
  • You can keep walking to any desired distance. 

Squat jumps 

When we talk about regular squats, it's a bodyweight move that targets your lower body. You simply need to add a jump to it and it will become an amazing cardio workout.

  • Your feet will be kept shoulder-wide apart, bend your knees, and squat.
  • Swing your arms upward and jump.
  • Land gently and repeat.

Final words

Cardio workouts are essential for your health and fitness, it helps to increase your blood flow and your heart pump faster, which means more oxygen throughout your body. This, in turn, keeps your heart and lungs healthy. Perform these cardio workouts at home and stay fit! In case of any confusion, you can connect with an online personal trainer to guide you.
